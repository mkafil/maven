SPC - Sharjah Publishing City Free Zone

SPC Free Zone (Sharjah Publishing City) is the world’s first free zone dedicated to publishing, media, and creative industries. Located in Sharjah, SPC is an ideal hub for writers, publishers, content creators, and digital businesses. SPC offers 100% foreign ownership, a fast business setup, and visa packages tailored for media and publishing businesses.

Key Benefits of SPC: ✅ Publishing & Media-Focused – The first free zone for content creators and publishers ✅ 100% Foreign Ownership – Full control over your company ✅ Flexible Visa Packages – Options for freelancers, entrepreneurs, and companies ✅ Strategic Location – Situated in Sharjah, close to Dubai ✅ Fast & Affordable Setup – Quick licensing process
