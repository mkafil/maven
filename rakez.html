RAKEZ - Designated Zone Private Office

RAKEZ (Ras Al Khaimah Economic Zone) is one of the most affordable free zones in the UAE, offering 100% foreign ownership, zero corporate tax, and cost-effective business setup solutions. Located in Ras Al Khaimah, RAKEZ is designed for startups, SMEs, and large enterprises looking for a business-friendly environment with world-class infrastructure. RAKEZ is ideal for manufacturing, trading, e-commerce, services, and freelancing, thanks to its low-cost business setup, strategic location near major ports, and flexible office solutions. Whether you're a small business or a multinational corporation, RAKEZ makes it easy to establish and grow in the UAE.

Key Benefits of RAKEZ: ✅ Low-Cost Business Setup – One of the most affordable free zones in the UAE ✅ 100% Foreign Ownership – Full control over your business ✅ Diverse Business Activities – Industrial, commercial, e-commerce, and service licenses available ✅ Strategic Location – Close to seaports, highways, and international trade routes ✅ Flexible Office & Warehouse Solutions – Options from virtual offices to industrial land
