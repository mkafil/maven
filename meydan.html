Why Choose Meydan Free Zone for Your Business?

Meydan Free Zone offers a unique opportunity for businesses to establish themselves in one of the most prestigious and dynamic locations in Dubai. As part of the Meydan Group, which is known for its world-class developments and strategic importance, the Free Zone offers an ideal environment for entrepreneurs, startups, and established companies looking to expand and thrive in the UAE and beyond

Here's why Meydan Free Zone is the perfect destination to incorporate your business:

  • Location

    • Strategic Location in the Heart of Dubai Meydan Free Zone is ideally situated in the heart of Dubai, providing businesses with exceptional access to key commercial, retail, and entertainment hubs in the city. The free zone is located just minutes away from Dubai International Airport and the city’s major highways, ensuring seamless connectivity to global markets. This strategic location makes it easy for companies to reach both regional and international clients.

  • World-Class Infrastructure

    • Meydan Free Zone is part of the Meydan Group’s world-class developments, offering state-of-the-art infrastructure and facilities. Whether you need office space, industrial units, or warehouses, Meydan Free Zone provides flexible solutions that can be tailored to your business needs. The modern design and high-tech facilities ensure that you have everything you need to operate efficiently and professionally.

  • 100% Foreign Ownership

    • As part of the UAE’s free zone initiative, Meydan Free Zone allows 100% foreign ownership, meaning you can retain complete control over your business. With no need for a local sponsor, investors can benefit from full autonomy in decision-making, ensuring your business runs exactly as you envision.

  • Favorable Tax Regime

    • Meydan Free Zone offers one of the most attractive tax environments in the UAE. Businesses benefit from zero corporate tax for up to 50 years, no personal income tax, and no VAT on the majority of services. This allows companies to maximize profits, reduce operational costs, and ensure long-term financial stability.

  • Flexible Company Structures

    • Meydan Free Zone provides a wide range of flexible business structures to meet your specific needs. Whether you are looking to set up a sole establishment, a branch of an international company, or a limited liability company, the free zone offers multiple options that allow you to operate in the UAE with ease.

  • Seamless Business Setup

    • Setting up a business in Meydan Free Zone is a straightforward and efficient process. The free zone's dedicated team provides personalized support and handles all aspects of company registration, from obtaining your trade license to visa processing. With fast-track procedures and minimal bureaucracy, you can get your business up and running in no time.

  • Access to Global Markets

    • With its strategic location and world-class connectivity, Meydan Free Zone provides unparalleled access to international markets. Whether you’re exporting products, offering services, or engaging in cross-border trade, Meydan Free Zone allows you to expand your reach to a global audience.

  • Competitive Pricing

    • Meydan Free Zone offers highly competitive pricing for company formation, office spaces, and other services. This ensures that entrepreneurs and small businesses can take advantage of a premium location and top-tier facilities without breaking the bank. With cost-effective business solutions, Meydan Free Zone is the perfect place for companies looking for value and growth.

  • Access to a Diverse Talent Pool

    • Dubai is home to a diverse and highly skilled workforce, and Meydan Free Zone makes it easy for you to tap into this talent pool. Whether you're looking for local professionals or international experts, the free zone offers access to a wide range of skilled employees from various industries, including finance, technology, healthcare, and more.

  • Dedicated Support Services

    • Meydan Free Zone offers a range of tailored services to support your business. From administrative assistance to legal and financial guidance, the free zone’s team is committed to helping your business grow and succeed. Their proactive approach ensures that you have the resources and support you need at every stage of your business journey.

  • Visas and Residence Permits

    • Meydan Free Zone offers a hassle-free visa process, allowing you and your employees to obtain residence permits quickly and efficiently. The zone also provides dependents' visas, making it easier to bring family members into the UAE as part of your business setup.

  • Innovative and Dynamic Environment

    • Meydan Free Zone is home to a diverse range of industries, including technology, finance, logistics, and trade. This dynamic environment fosters innovation, collaboration, and growth. Being part of a community of forward-thinking businesses allows you to network, share ideas, and create new opportunities for your company.
