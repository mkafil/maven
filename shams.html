Why Choose SHAMS Free Zone for Your Business Expansion?

SHAMS (Sharjah Media City) is a leading business hub in the UAE, providing a world-class platform for entrepreneurs, startups, and established businesses looking to grow and innovate. Located in the thriving emirate of Sharjah, SHAMS offers a range of benefits tailored to businesses in diverse sectors, from media and entertainment to technology and consulting. Here’s why SHAMS is the right choice for your business setup:

Highlights & Benefits

  • A Premier Location for Strategic Growth

    • SHAMS Free Zone is strategically located in Sharjah, close to Dubai, offering direct access to one of the region's most vital commercial ecosystems. The zone benefits from Sharjah’s thriving economy, ease of access to major transportation routes, and proximity to Dubai's business hub. This advantageous location ensures that your business can seamlessly tap into regional and international markets.

  • Simplified and Efficient Business Setup

    • Starting your business in SHAMS is a quick and straightforward process. The free zone offers a seamless, hassle-free setup experience, with fast-track processing times, minimal paperwork, and affordable setup packages. With SHAMS, you can have your company registered and operational in a matter of days, empowering you to focus on what truly matters: growing your business.

  • 100% Ownership and Control

    • As an investor in SHAMS, you can benefit from 100% foreign ownership of your business. This offers complete control and autonomy, without the need for a local sponsor or partner. With full ownership rights, you can make key decisions independently, retain all profits, and ensure your business operates exactly as you envision.

  • Tax Benefits and Cost-Effectiveness

    • SHAMS Free Zone offers a highly attractive financial structure, with numerous benefits designed to help businesses maximize profitability. Enjoy zero corporate tax, no personal income tax, and no customs duties on import/export activities. SHAMS also provides affordable office space solutions, making it an ideal option for both new startups and expanding enterprises.

  • Wide Range of Business Activities

    • SHAMS offers flexibility in terms of the types of businesses you can operate. Whether you are involved in media, technology, consulting, e-commerce, or manufacturing, SHAMS supports a diverse range of business activities with customizable licensing options. This flexibility allows businesses from virtually any sector to thrive in a dynamic, innovation-driven environment.

  • Fast and Easy Visa Processing

    • Setting up in SHAMS comes with the added benefit of simplified visa procedures. Whether you need a visa for yourself, your employees, or your family members, SHAMS ensures that the visa application process is efficient and streamlined. With SHAMS, you can quickly secure UAE residence visas for all stakeholders involved in your business.

  • Modern Infrastructure for Business Excellence

    • SHAMS provides state-of-the-art office facilities, designed to meet the needs of modern businesses. From flexible coworking spaces to fully serviced office units, SHAMS offers a range of options to accommodate businesses of all sizes. Additionally, the zone is equipped with advanced telecommunications and IT infrastructure, ensuring that your business operations run smoothly and efficiently.

  • Access to a Diverse Talent Pool

    • Located in a region known for its multicultural workforce, SHAMS offers businesses access to a wide range of skilled professionals. Whether you need expertise in media, tech, finance, or any other industry, the UAE is home to a diverse talent pool, making it easier to hire the right people for your business. SHAMS’ proximity to educational institutions and training centers further enhances the ease of recruitment.

  • Excellent Connectivity

    • RAK Free Zone is strategically located near major transport hubs, including the Ras Al Khaimah International Airport and the Saqr Port. This connectivity allows easy access to global markets, with fast transportation options for goods and personnel.

  • A Supportive Business Community

    • SHAMS provides an environment that fosters collaboration and innovation. As part of the free zone, you’ll join a vibrant community of like-minded professionals, entrepreneurs, and global enterprises. With regular networking events, industry seminars, and workshops, SHAMS ensures that businesses have the resources and connections needed to expand their networks, collaborate on projects, and explore new opportunities.

  • Flexible Office Solutions

    • SHAMS offers flexible office solutions that cater to businesses of all sizes. Whether you need a dedicated office, shared coworking space, or a virtual office, SHAMS provides the infrastructure you need to establish a professional presence. With scalable office options, businesses can easily adapt to changing needs as they grow.

  • Comprehensive Legal and Financial Support

    • SHAMS offers a comprehensive range of services to assist with your business’s legal and financial needs. Whether you require assistance with company formation, intellectual property protection, corporate governance, or banking, SHAMS provides the support and guidance necessary to navigate the regulatory landscape and ensure your business remains compliant and successful.
