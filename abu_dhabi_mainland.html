Why Choose Abu Dhabi Mainland for Your Business?

Access to a Flourishing Local and International Market Abu Dhabi Mainland licenses grant you unrestricted access to the entire UAE market, including the rapidly growing local and international markets. With a robust economy driven by sectors like oil, gas, finance, technology, tourism, and more, establishing a business here offers vast potential to expand and engage in both local and global trade.

Highlights & Benefits

  • Flexibility in Business Operations

    • Abu Dhabi Mainland licensing allows for diverse business activities across multiple sectors. Whether you’re in retail, hospitality, construction, media, healthcare, or technology, Abu Dhabi offers flexibility and the freedom to choose from a wide range of business activities. This allows entrepreneurs to tailor their business model and operations based on their specific needs and goals.

  • Tax Benefits

    • RAK Free Zone offers an attractive tax regime, with zero corporate tax for up to 50 years, ensuring significant savings for your business. Additionally, there are no personal income taxes, import/export duties, or capital gains taxes. This creates a highly favorable environment for businesses seeking to minimize operational costs and maximize profits.

  • No Restrictions on Market Access

    • No Restrictions on Market Access Unlike free zones, businesses operating under Abu Dhabi Mainland licenses can freely conduct business within the local UAE market, engage with government entities, and even work with other mainland businesses. This flexibility enables you to build strong local relationships and develop long-term partnerships, driving your business success and growth.

  • Full Ownership Rights for Foreign Investors

    • Full Ownership Rights for Foreign Investors Abu Dhabi Mainland offers 100% foreign ownership for businesses, allowing you to retain full control and ownership without the need for a local sponsor. This gives you complete autonomy over business decisions, profits, and operational strategies, offering a high degree of independence and security for foreign investors.

  • Reputation and Credibility

    • Reputation and Credibility Having a business established in Abu Dhabi Mainland adds credibility and prestige to your company. As the UAE’s capital, Abu Dhabi is recognized for its strong regulatory environment, stable economy, and world-class infrastructure. Being part of this dynamic city enhances your brand's reputation, helping you gain trust with local and international clients and partners

  • Ideal Location for Strategic Expansion

    • Abu Dhabi is centrally located, offering easy access to major markets in the Middle East, Africa, and Asia. Its strategic position is enhanced by well-developed transport infrastructure, including the world-class Abu Dhabi International Airport and sea ports, allowing for efficient logistics and seamless access to global business opportunities.

  • Access to Highly Skilled Talent

    • Abu Dhabi’s diverse and skilled workforce provides businesses with access to a wide pool of talent across various industries. Whether you're seeking experts in finance, technology, engineering, or any other field, you can tap into a professional labor force that is highly educated, innovative, and experienced.

  • Tax-Free Incentives

    • One of the significant advantages of establishing your business in Abu Dhabi Mainland is its attractive tax incentives. Businesses in Abu Dhabi can enjoy zero corporate tax and no personal income tax. Additionally, there are no restrictions on capital repatriation, giving your business financial freedom and flexibility.

  • Sustainable Business Environment

    • Abu Dhabi has made significant strides in sustainability, promoting green initiatives and eco-friendly practices. If your business has a focus on innovation, sustainability, or environmental responsibility, setting up in Abu Dhabi aligns with the UAE’s vision of creating a sustainable future. The city offers various incentives for businesses that support sustainability and green technologies.
