ABU DHABI MAINLAND - Designated Zone Private Office

RAKEZ, located in Ras Al Khaimah, UAE, is a leading economic zone and business licensing authority. It offers tailored solutions for company formation in the UAE, catering to both free zone and mainland businesses. Home to over 18,000 companies across more than 50 sectors, RAKEZ supports businesses of all sizes, from startups and SMEs to large industrial operations. As one of the region's largest economic zones, it facilitates business setup with a variety of licenses, residence visa services, and customizable facilities. Beyond licensing and infrastructure, RAKEZ provides valuable support services, including accounting, translation, banking assistance, recruitment, procurement, and marketing.
