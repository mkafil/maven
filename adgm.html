ADGM - Abu Dhabi Global Market

ADGM (Abu Dhabi Global Market) is a leading international financial center (IFC) located on Al Maryah Island, Abu Dhabi. Designed to be the financial powerhouse of the UAE, ADGM follows English Common Law and is home to banks, fintech companies, asset managers, and professional service firms. As a globally recognized business hub, ADGM offers 100% foreign ownership, tax-friendly policies, and a business-friendly regulatory framework. It is the preferred choice for financial institutions, legal firms, and wealth management companies looking to expand in the Middle East.

Key Benefits of ADGM: ✅ International Financial Center – A global hub for banks, fintech, and legal firms ✅ English Common Law – The only financial free zone in the UAE following English law ✅ 100% Foreign Ownership – Full business control with no local partner required ✅ No Corporate Tax – Business-friendly financial regulations ✅ Strong Regulatory Framework – A trusted environment for global investors
